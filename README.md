# IOT Club

## Objective

The objective of this club is to introduce student to IoT and give them a starting point to build real world solutions.

We also strive to make the club as much fun as possible, challenge students to creativity, low cost, meet health and safety requirements and scalable, extendable and modular IoT projects.

The project will be from building the hardware, firmware and then integration into a IoT dashboard.
